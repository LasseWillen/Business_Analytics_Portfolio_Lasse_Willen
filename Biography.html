About Me

Born and raised in Germany, I took my first steps into the working world by diving into roles as varied as a tennis coach at summer camps and a waiter, all while finishing up my A-Levels in Biberach, Baden-Württemberg. This mix of experiences taught me early on about the value of hard work and adaptability.

My academic journey then led me to ESB Reutlingen to study International Management, where I developed a deep interest in operations and lean management. It wasn't just about the textbooks; I got to apply what I learned by working as a process optimizer for the Reutlingen Health District during the Covid-19 pandemic. This experience was eye-opening, pushing me to think on my feet and find solutions in the most challenging situations.

Sustainability has always been a priority for me, and landing a role at SAP's Sustainability office felt like the perfect fit. It was an incredible opportunity to align my career with my values.

My journey continued at DCU, where I pursued the second half of my studies, further sharpening my skills and focus, which is where I currently major in Data Analytics. During my studies at DCU, I took a job at the green unicorn startup Enpal in Berlin, which was a game-changer for me. Working in Venture Development I was part of a team enabling a solar company to make real strides toward sustainability.

This experience established my career ambitions: I want to be at the forefront of a startup driving towards sustainable goals, leveraging my background in operations and my growing expertise in data analytics. I hope to make a profound impact in this career.
